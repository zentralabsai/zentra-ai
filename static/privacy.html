Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Kazfen ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website kazfen.com and provides AI-powered lead management services for roofing contractors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our services, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Property address and location
  • Roofing service details (roof type, issue description, urgency)
  • Insurance claim information
  • Preferred inspection timing and budget
  • Messages and communications through our chat, SMS, and phone systems

We also automatically collect certain technical information including IP addresses, browser type, and usage data when you visit our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Connect you with qualified roofing specialists in your area
  • Schedule and confirm roof inspection appointments
  • Send SMS notifications about your inquiry, bookings, and weather alerts
  • Qualify and score leads to ensure timely follow-up
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Communicate with you about our services

4. SMS Communications

By submitting your phone number through our website, you consent to receive SMS messages from Kazfen related to your roofing inquiry. These messages may include appointment confirmations, follow-up messages, storm damage alerts, and service updates.

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your inquiry.

5. AI-Powered Services

Kazfen uses artificial intelligence to qualify leads, make automated phone calls, and provide chat-based assistance. Your conversations with our AI systems are processed to provide roofing service recommendations and are stored to improve service quality. Phone calls may be recorded for quality assurance purposes.

6. Weather and Location Data

We use your location information to check real-time weather conditions and severe weather alerts in your area. This helps us prioritize urgent roofing needs and alert you to potential storm damage. We use third-party weather services (Tomorrow.io and the National Weather Service) to obtain this data.

7. Information Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Roofing contractors in your area who can service your request
  • Service providers who assist in operating our platform (Twilio for SMS/calls, Google for calendar scheduling, Stripe for payments)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@kazfen.com.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of SMS communications by replying STOP
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

11. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services including Twilio (communications), Google Calendar (scheduling), Stripe (payments), Tomorrow.io (weather data), and the National Weather Service (severe weather alerts). Each service has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
